Music

I've always loved music. I've been singing for as long as I remember. Then in 6th grade I joined Orchestra. I played the cello for 8 years. During that time I got into choir my sophmore year of high school. I found my place. I sang in as many ensembles as I could, and started studying voice privately with Sarah Whitlatch. Then when I came to Montana State University I was able to sing in Montanans, the auditioned chamber choir. This led me to sing in Voces Lumines and eventually join Professor Elizabeth Croys studio!
